7 AXAGroup’s contribution to the Sustainable Development Goals of the United Nations
In 2018, the Group developed a strategic framework to identify its commitments to the UN Sustainable Development Goals (17 SDGs see https://sdgs.un.org) based on its risk analysis expertise. In 2019, the Group aligned its sustainability strategic objectives with the 8 main SDGs with respect to which the Group’s initiatives have a significant impact (approved as part of the 2021-2023 Group sustainability strategy).
